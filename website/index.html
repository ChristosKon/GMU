Are you ready for the best week of your life?

Ten months have passed since Greece Me Up, the exchange that changed once and forever the summer of all EESTECers. 90 EESTECers met Greek history at Vergina, visited beautiful Edessa and its waterfalls, tasted the delicious Greek cuisine, sunbathed at Posidi, and partied like there was no tomorrow.

LC Athens keeps its promises so, this summer get ready for Greece Me Up Vol.2 , all fresh and revamped. The Queen of North, Thesaloniki will be our destination. Day trips at gorgeous places of northern Greece and a visit at one of the biggest waterparks in Europe will make the hot days cooler.

You want more? What about 3 days and nights by the seaside surrounded with eestec spirit. Yes, we are talking about our IMW, which will take place this July, as a conclusion for our exchange. Many activities, sun, and sea will be our company.

You are not allowed to miss it!
