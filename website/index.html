It's not rumours; it will be the best summer-week ever!

LC Athens keeps its promises so, this summer get ready for Greece Me Up Vol.2 , all fresh and revamped. The Queen of North, Thessaloniki will be our destination. Day trips at gorgeous places of northern Greece and a visit at one of the biggest waterparks in Europe will make the hot days cooler.
